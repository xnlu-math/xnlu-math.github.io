I am currently an Associate Professor at the Department of Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering, Gifu University, Japan.

My research interests include discrete mathematics (combinatorics and combinatorial algorithms), coding theory, cryptography, and related interdisciplinary studies, with an emphasis on statistics and theoretical computer science. I also explore SAT and constraint programming techniques for solving combinatorial problems computationally. Recently, my research has focused on group testing and combinatorial interaction testing.
