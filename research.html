Research Interests

Information Science (Coding Theory, Cryptography)

  • Algebraic codes and combinatorial codes
  • Secret sharing schemes and authentication codes
  • Pseudorandom binary sequences
  • Compressed sensing and its applications

Mathematics (Combinatorics, Algebra)

  • Combinatorial design theory
  • Finite fields and their applications
  • Finite geometries and their applications
  • Graphs associated with finite groups (e.g. Cayley graphs)
  • Graph decomposition and graph labeling
  • Probabilistic methods in combinatorics

Statistics (Design of Experiments, Discrete Data Analysis)

  • Optimal designs of experiments
  • Design of computer experiments
  • Statistical analysis for categorical data

Interdisciplinary studies involving the above

  • Combinatorial group testing and related combinatorial search problems
  • Combinatorial interaction testing and its applications in software engineering
  • Algorithmic approach for combinatorial designs and related structures
